- The Story of Letflix -

Letflix is a streaming house for watching movies, dramas, documentary, and much more. We made this application because we want to make it easier for people to watch movies or whatever they want wherever and whenever. With Letflix, people don't have to go far to go to the cinema to watch the movies they want. Netflix also provides a cheaper price compared to having to come to the cinema. Our members control what they want to watch, when they want it, with no ads, in one simple subscription. We’re streaming in more than 30 languages and 190 countries.

- Company's Culture -

Entertainment is a basic human need that can change our feelings. Letflix wants to turn your world into a world of entertainment. We want to bring you more laughter, more empathy, and more joy. To get there, we have an amazing and unusual employee culture. This document is about that culture.
